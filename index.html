How Do You Face Change?

In Who Moved My Cheese?, four characters react to the same change in four very different ways. None of us is purely one character — but most of us lean toward one when the Cheese disappears. This 12-question self-check reveals your dominant change style, shows your full mix, and gives you practical tips to handle change better.

🐭 Sniff The Anticipator
Senses change early and starts moving before others notice.
🐭 Scurry The Mover
Stops analyzing and just acts — fast, energetic, decisive.
🧑 Haw The Learner
Resists first, then grows through fear and adapts.
🧑 Hem The Resister
Denies the change and waits for things to go back to normal.
